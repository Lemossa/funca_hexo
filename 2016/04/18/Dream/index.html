Dream

Having a dream
A dream called life

Beautiful, that I would not wake up
Bitter, that it’d better not be life

Here

Where I tell you what I feel

Promise

After breaking a promise,
I dare to make no more

Past

I thought the past has passed,
but it’s living
I thought the past has passed,
but it’s haunting
I thought the past has passed,
but it’s beating

I thought the past would not pass,
yet it died

Red lips and rosy cheeks

but every once in awhile, you find someone who’s iridescent.
